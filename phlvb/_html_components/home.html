Welcome to Philly Volleyball

Philly Volleyball is the hub for adult volleyball across the Philadelphia. Our mission is to provide an inclusive and welcoming community for volleyball players of all skill levels, while promoting the growth and development of the sport in the Philadelphia area. Whether you’re a beginner or a college star, by yourself or have a team, we have ways to play.

Old School Rules

Back in the late 90s there were a variety of rule changes made to outdoor volleyball. In Pennsylvania, we have the Pottstown Rumble, the largest grass volleyball tournament each year. Most grass events in the area use a rule set similar to the “Old School Rules” of the Pottstown rumble.

  • Large Court (30x60)

  • No let serves

  • Block is not a touch

  • No open hand receives (unless hard driven)

  • No open-hand tips, sets over the net or directional blocking

  • Some events may use side out scoring.
