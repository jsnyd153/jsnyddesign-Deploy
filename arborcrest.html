website design

to attract new tenants to its renovated corporate campus, Spear Street Capital needed a new marketing materials anchored by a dynamic digital site.

as part of building out a larger suite of marketing materials for arborcrest, i was tasked with designing a website to promote their campus and give as much insight into the space as possible, while focusing on the ability to customize office spaces. to accomplish this, as emphasis was put on leveraging interactive and video elements. the interactive “A” featured at the top of the site draws inspiration from the real life pixel-flip board on the main campus to bring the physical campus to life online.
